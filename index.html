Covid Connect Nepal

Detail

In mid-2021, I volunteered in Covid Connect Nepal(CCN), a subsidiary body under Covid Crisis Management of Nepal. It was probably the best thing I did in my whole life. Waking up till midnight and arranging beds, ICU, and oxygen cylinders for covid patients through phone calls were worth doing. It wasn't an uncomplicated task. We had to face the deaths of many people all the time and personally get scolded by hospital call representatives for calling repeatedly. I learned to work as a team and continually give my best in the darkest of times.

I made many new connections with crystal clear hearts. My experience in CCN taught me how important one's life can be. Although my time and contribution to CCN were only for few months, my perspective of life got broader, and my journey continued ahead with being a better me.
